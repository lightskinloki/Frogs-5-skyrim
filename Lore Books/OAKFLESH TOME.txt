PREFACE: THE ARCHITECTS, THE MORTALS, AND THE BONES
The School of Alteration is the most academic, the most dangerous, and the most misunderstood of the Six Great Schools. It is not about creation or destruction; it is about revision.
The first lesson of Alteration is that the physical world—Mundus—is structured by the sacrificed residue of the Aedra, who became the Earth Bones. They are the laws of gravity, tensile strength, inertia, and biological decay. Every mortal existence is predicated upon the constancy of these Bones.
When a Novice casts Oakflesh, they are not adding a layer of Magicka. They are issuing a complex instruction to the Earth Bones governing their flesh: For sixty seconds, treat this living keratin and collagen as if it were the densest, most resilient heartwood of a mature Oak. This instruction is temporary because the residual will of the Aedra—the very Aedroth—is ceaseless, constantly working to Reassert the Pre-Established Order.
The difference between a successful spell and an instantaneous Ontological Detachment is the caster’s Conviction. The caster must possess the mental fortitude to hold two opposing realities in mind simultaneously: that they are a concrete, finite being, and that they are also a mutable, temporary construct of mutable laws.
This primer, therefore, is not merely a spell book; it is a spiritual and technical guide to the fundamental act of Defiance by Subtlety.
I. REALITY AND THE FALSEHOOD: THE EARTH BONES DOCTRINE
I.A. The Investment of the Spirits and the Nature of Magicka Flow
Magicka, which flows from the Sun (Magnus) and the stars (Magna-Ge), is the raw, unformed power of creation. However, in Alteration, this power is not used as a weapon (Destruction) or a lure (Conjuration). It is used as a Metaphysical Solvent.
When Oakflesh is channeled, the Magicka saturates the Primary Saturation Zone (the epidermal and sub-dermal layers). This saturated layer becomes the Aetherial Matrix. This matrix does two things:
1. It dampens the local influence of the Earth Bones governing the caster’s physical form.
2. It replaces that influence with the structure of the Oak Template defined by the True Name.
The Earth Bones, being non-sentient but universally consistent forces, temporarily concede to the localized, internally-anchored, superior Magicka-force (the spell). The choice of the Oak template is critical: it is a high-stability, low-resistance organic structure, requiring minimal Magicka to stabilize the divergence.
I.B. The Concept of Structural Divergence (The Delta-State)
The moment the spell takes hold, the caster enters a Delta-State—a temporary reality where the physical properties of their body differ from the rest of Mundus. The danger here lies in the Delta-Shock, the instantaneous cognitive dissonance caused by sensing the body's new properties while the mind insists upon the old.
* Delta-Shock Mitigation: The Novice must internalize the mantra: "The skin is wood, the mind is stone. The core remains." This focused grounding prevents the mind from wandering during the ontological shift, which is the primary cause of Ontological Anxiety in Novices.
* The Reversion Force: The Earth Bones immediately begin exerting a powerful, continuous force to collapse the Aetherial Matrix and return the caster to the Base-State. This is the Reversion Force. The spell’s duration is a direct function of the caster’s ability to resist this force using sustained, low-level Magicka flow and high mental conviction.
II. THE EARTH BONE ANCHOR: KYNAR-ETH'S DOMAIN
II.A. Kynareth’s Influence and Organic Templates
The Alteration School deals heavily with Kynareth's Domain—the laws of organic life, weather, wind, and movement. The Oakflesh spell is the earliest practical lesson in respecting and manipulating this Domain.
* Why Oak? The Temperate Stability Factor (TSF): The Oak template is chosen for the Novice because it possesses a Temperate Stability Factor (TSF) of 7.2, a high measure of resistance to both Fire and Frost (Elemental Spasms) while maintaining biological consistency. Higher-rank spells like Ebonyflesh (Apprentice/Adept) use inorganic templates that have a TSF approaching 9.5, but require exponentially more Magicka and far superior mental shielding to prevent the inorganic infusion from corrupting the soft tissues.
II.B. The Risk of Internal Lignification and Organ Spasm
The Magicka flow must be precisely contained within the Primary Saturation Zone (skin and immediate muscle tissue). Due to the body’s highly interconnected cellular structure, any bleed-over of the Magicka-solvent into the internal organs is catastrophic.
* Cardiac Lignification: Unintentional alteration of the cardiac muscle tissue, causing instantaneous hardening and failure. This is often triggered by poor breathing (lack of Magicka Pacing) or a failed Subtle Clasp Mudra.
* Pulmonary Calcification: The alteration of the soft, porous tissue of the lungs into a woody or stony template. The lungs retain their shape but lose the ability to exchange air, leading to rapid suffocation. This is the result of insufficient control over the upper torso saturation flow.
The critical Novice check for successful casting is not the sight of the shimmer, but the feeling of constriction without pain. Pain indicates internal bleed-over and the spell must be aborted immediately.
III. REVIEW OF NOVICE PRACTICES (ALTERATION-SPECIFIC)
III.A. Magicka Flow, The Internal Conduit, and Retaliation
The internal flow of Magicka for Alteration is a two-phase process: Draw and Diffusion.
1. Draw Phase (The Inward Visualization): Magicka is drawn through the palms and soles of the feet, visualized as a clear, highly viscous liquid being pulled toward the body's core (The Anchor Point, located just below the sternum). This process should feel cool and calming.
2. Diffusion Phase (Saturation): The anchored Magicka is gently diffused outward, targeting the dermal and sub-dermal layers. The visualization here is crucial: The Magicka does not rush; it seeps. It must fill the desired zone evenly, like water soaking into dry moss. Any sensation of pulsing or abrupt surge during diffusion indicates a breach of control and imminent Elemental Spasm.
Retaliation: The Backflow Error: The greatest Novice error is attempting to reuse the Magicka used in the spell. This is impossible. Once the spell ends, the Magicka is permanently changed by its work, becoming inert Aetherial Slag. Attempting to channel this back into the core is an act of Magicka Poisoning, resulting in Conduit Scarring and debilitating pain. The Magicka must be allowed to dissipate entirely back into the Aether.
III.B. The Mental Shield and Ontological Anxiety
The purpose of the Mental Shield is to reinforce the self against the psychological assault of the Delta-State. The very act of changing your nature is a confrontation with the limits of your own reality.
* The Thirty-Second Paradox (Reinforced): This exercise is mandatory and non-negotiable. It reinforces the duality of self:
   1. The Finite Truth: “I, [Caster’s Name and Lineage], am anchored in this moment, held by Akatosh’s Time and the Bones of Nirn.” (Focus on the weight of your feet and the slow beat of your heart).
   2. The Mutability Lie: “I am an editable structure, a willing divergence, capable of changing the unchangeable with conviction.” (Focus on the potential energy in your hands and the flow of Magicka in your core).
* Failure: A failure to maintain this paradox leads to Ontological Detachment (OD-1). The most common symptom is the loss of one's own sense of identity, often leading to immediate and complete cessation of being, as the mind collapses into the truth that all reality is an option.
IV. PREPARATION: THE PHYSICAL AND THE META-PHYSICAL
IV.A. The Posture of Conviction and Mudra Refinement
The physical body acts as a focus lens for the internal Magicka flow. Any unnecessary tension acts as a disruptive tremor.
1. Stance and Grounding: Stand square, feet shoulder-width apart. Knees slightly unlocked. Imagine your spine is the Temporal Anchor, perfectly straight and rooted.
2. Mudra (Hand Gesture): The Subtle Clasp (Refined). Extend both hands forward. Palms facing inward, cupped as if holding a small, polished stone. The distance between the palms must be exactly three finger-widths—no more, no less. This specific gap creates a natural, low-resistance psychic circuit for the Magicka to flow. The Novice must hold this position without tremor for the entire duration of the Diffusion Phase.
3. Breathing and Magicka Pacing (The 4-4-8 Cycle):
   * Inhale (4 Counts): Draw Phase. Magicka enters through the extremities, moving to the Anchor Point.
   * Hold (4 Counts): Anchor Phase. Mental Shield is engaged. Paradoxical statements are affirmed.
   * Exhale (8 Counts): Diffusion Phase. Magicka is slowly, gently pushed from the core outward into the Saturation Zone. The True Name is mentally chanted during this exhale.
IV.B. The Environmental Condition: Kynareth's View
The ideal environment minimizes external Magicka interference.
* Optimal: A quiet, natural forest, preferably near a mature Oak tree (TSF 7.2 template source). The existing "Earth Bone structure" is cleaner.
* Sub-Optimal: Urban environments (residual chaos), Dwemer ruins (metallic, inorganic, and often hostile Magicka residue), or deep underground. Casting in these areas requires a 20% increase in Magicka expenditure to stabilize the Matrix and triples the risk of Magicka Instability and Elemental Spasm.
V. THE ARCANE THEORY: LIGNIFICATION BY WILL
V.A. The Mechanics of Property Transfer: The Three Vector Shift
Oakflesh does not create external armor; it redefines the intrinsic properties of the tissue. This transfer is achieved via the Three Vector Shift within the Aetherial Matrix:
1. Tensile Strength (Internal, Cohesion Vector): The Magicka dramatically increases the molecular density of the collagen fibers, making the skin highly resistant to tearing, piercing, or slashing.
2. Kinetic Absorption (External, Impact Vector): The matrix creates a temporary, sub-dermal layer that uses the Magicka to dynamically spread and dissipate blunt force impact across a wider surface area, mimicking the shock-absorbing quality of resilient wood fiber. This is why the spell feels like tight constriction.
3. Elemental Resistance (Surface, Boundary Vector): The template imparts Oak's modest resistance profile. The caster gains a low-level, temporary resistance to non-physical damage (Fire, Frost, Shock) due to the dense cellular structure of the wood template.
V.B. Understanding Spell Decay and Forced Reversion
The Earth Bones constantly fight the spell. As Magicka is depleted or Conviction wanes, the Reversion Force grows exponentially stronger.
* The Reversion Point: The precise moment the Reversion Force exceeds the caster's remaining Conviction and Magicka flow is the Reversion Point. The Aetherial Matrix collapses instantly.
* Violent Reversion: If the caster attempts to force the spell to continue beyond the Reversion Point, the Earth Bones will violently Reassert the Pre-Established Order. This manifests as debilitating muscular trauma, including severe cramps, skin tearing, and potential bone fracture as the body violently snaps back to its Base-State. This is not injury from magic, but injury from the laws of physics reasserting themselves with force.
VI. SPELL DESCRIPTION: THE SUBTLETY OF CHANGE
VI.A. Objective and Result: The Shimmer of Defiance
Spell Name: Oakflesh (Novice-Rank Alteration)
Objective: To temporarily reinforce the physical structure of the caster's skin and soft tissue, granting a defensive rating equivalent to a thin layer of mature Oak heartwood.
Defensive Rating: TSF 7.2 (Equivalent to +40 points of temporary protection in standard defensive indexing).
Duration: Standard Novice duration (typically 60 seconds of stable sustain, max 90 seconds).
Magicka Cost: Sustained Low-Level Channeling (High initial cost for matrix establishment, lower continuous cost for resistance to Reversion Force).
Visual Effect (The Shimmer): A visible, yet subtle, shimmering, brass-green glow across the Primary Saturation Zone. The color is derived from the elemental confluence of Magicka and Kynareth's Domain. The skin will feel abnormally hard, possessing a wood-like texture, and the sound of a fingernail scraping the surface will be a low, dull thud.
VI.B. The Sensation of Defiance (The Ontological Chill)
Upon successful casting, the Novice experiences the Ontological Chill—the physical manifestation of the psychic shockwave caused by defying the Earth Bones.
* Symptom: A momentary, intense, cold shudder that begins at the core and radiates outward through the newly altered tissue. This is the body registering the Delta-State.
* Action: The Novice must immediately stabilize this sensation by focusing entirely on the feeling of the tight constriction (Kinetic Absorption Vector). Any lapse of focus in this moment allows the Ontological Anxiety to take root. Successfully processing the Chill confirms the Mental Shield is intact.

VIII. CONTRAINDICATIONS AND WARNINGS (ALTERATION)
VIII.A. Cross-School Interference and Elemental Spasms
The integrity of the Aetherial Matrix is delicate. Introducing any other Magicka structure (even minor Destruction or Restoration) will cause a collapse.
* Destruction (Fire, Frost): Casting Firebolt while Oakflesh is active will almost guarantee a Thermal Lignification spasm. The fire energy corrupts the Oak template, causing the Magicka-infused skin to rapidly combust and char, resulting in severe burns.
* Restoration: Paradoxically, attempting minor healing on the affected limb during Oakflesh can cause the Magicka fields to violently oppose each other. The Restoration Magicka insists on the original, damaged Base-State, while the Alteration Magicka insists on the Delta-State. The resulting conflict causes intense, localized tissue pain and Magicka poisoning.
VIII.B. Over-Casting, Strain, and the Tenacity Limit
The Novice must strictly adhere to the temporal limits of the spell. Attempting to force the sustain past the 90-second Tenacity Limit is an act of violent confrontation with the Earth Bones.
* Magicka Implosion (Aetherial Collapse): When the spell is forced, the Reversion Force rapidly crushes the Magicka Conduit. The remaining Magicka is violently rejected and collapses inward, resulting in acute, temporary Magicka depletion and extreme pain. The caster is left paralyzed and unable to cast for several minutes.
* Permanent Joint Calcification: Sustained, reckless over-casting can lead to a residual, latent Lignification that hardens the fluid in the joints (elbows, knuckles, knees). This is the irreversible Tendon Calcification trauma, severely limiting mobility and requiring life-long Restoration treatments.
VIII.C. The Existential Warning (The End-Game of Alteration)
We conclude this instruction with the same warning we began with: Alteration is the most dangerous school of magic.
Every step forward in Alteration is a step closer to madness. The Adept learns that matter is negotiable (Ironflesh); the Expert learns that time and movement are debatable (Mass Paralysis); and the Master learns that existence itself is subjective.
The path ends in one of two ways: Mastery, or Ontological Detachment (OD-1). Upon realizing the fundamental falsehood of Mundus (CHIM), the mind, unable to reconcile the truth, rejects its own existence. The body is not killed; it simply ceases to be a subject of the Earth Bones, unraveling from reality. Your Conviction is the only thing keeping you tethered to Nirn.

This custody is mandated due to the catastrophic risk posed by an entity that has achieved a partial-CHIM state without the requisite wisdom—they are functionally unstable reality-warpers and an immediate threat to the structural integrity of Mundus.

The earliest systematized Alteration mastery belonged to the Ayleids (Heartland High Elves). Their power, fueled by their captured star-light prisms (Ayleid Wells), allowed them to achieve forms of Alteration now considered myth: Entropy Reversal and Geographic Levitation.

This is the most dangerous recorded use of Alteration.
* The Lesson: The Oakflesh spell, with its short duration and benign template, is a deliberate, highly-filtered remnant of this ancient power. The Novice must recognize that in their hands is a fraction of the power that reshaped the early Tamrielic landscape.
