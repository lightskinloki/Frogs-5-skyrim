THE SOVEREIGN'S GIFTS — GM MASTER
Ismara's power sheet: what she can do, and what it really costs.
GM-ONLY. The player gets "PLAYER HANDOUT - Sovereign Gifts.txt" (no spoilers).

Companion to: The Winter Ledger (the clock) and Npcs/Ismara (who she is).

=====================================================================
THE CONTEXT THAT MAKES THIS WORK (read first)
=====================================================================
These gifts are usable ONLY while ISMARA is driving (the extended swap, Ch.23 —
silver-white skin, straight hair). That single fact dictates every mechanic
below, and it is where my first draft was wrong:

  - There is NO "reaching inward," NO Integration Check, and NO "burn 1 Max FP to
    resist." Those are DAVINIA-driving mechanics (Players/Davinia): they model
    Davinia borrowing the Crown's power and her will resisting the rewrite.
    Davinia is not at the wheel. She cannot reach, roll, or refuse. She is
    submerged in the basement (Ch.23). Do not use those mechanics here.

  - While Ismara drives, the gifts are simply HERS. She wills them; they work.
    No roll, no failure, no veto. A queen's power does not hesitate.

THE REAL COST: the gifts run on DAVINIA. To fuel a greater or sovereign gift,
Ismara spends a piece of the submerged Davinia — a memory, a warmth, a part of
who she is — and the merger advances (CDI climbs). This reconciles cleanly with
the world tracker (the swap itself is cost-free in-fiction): HOLDING the wheel
costs nothing; USING the power is what consumes Davinia. The power is free to
wear and ruinous to use — and the player will want to use it.

THE META LEVER ("IRL CDI", Npcs/Ismara CURRENT TRUTH): the gifts are
deliberately, unfairly good so the PLAYER keeps choosing to wield Ismara. Wanting
the power is the trap; every indulgence spends Davinia. Make them a pleasure to
run.

=====================================================================
HOW THE COST WORKS (one rule for every gift)
=====================================================================
1. NO ROLL, NO VETO. Ismara is in control. She decides, she pays, it is done.
2. WHAT SHE SPENDS: greater gifts cost a piece of Davinia (a memory / a warmth);
   sovereign gifts cost a large piece (a relationship, a core memory). Lesser
   gifts cost essentially nothing — her resting competence.
3. CDI ADVANCES on every greater/sovereign use (track it; values per gift below).
   The player NEVER sees the number — only the memory going out, the silver
   climbing, Davinia getting fainter below.
4. THE BRAKE (re-anchoring, canon): the player may surface Davinia (cede the
   wheel) at any time; party warmth aimed at DAVINIA — Ylva, Danica, the bonds —
   helps her rise and hold (Winter Ledger V, Davinia re-anchor rule). But every
   gift already spent left less of her to bring back. Power now vs. how much of
   her remains.

THE CLIFF (0.5 — adapted from Winter Ledger II for the driving phase):
  CDI starts at 0.45. At 0.5 there is not enough Davinia left to ever surface —
  she is gone; only Ismara remains (the merger self-sustains, sliding toward Tier
  10). Because Davinia isn't present to be asked, the Winter Ledger's
  second-suppression "ask" becomes a STATED WARNING: before the gift that would
  cross 0.5, Ismara says the true thing plainly (she never lies, V.1) — that this
  one takes "the last of her," and she will not wake again. The player crosses
  knowing exactly what it costs. That is the one clean tell; deliver it ONCE, in
  her voice, never softened.

RETROACTIVE DREAD: in the moment, every cost feels small — a memory she "isn't
using," a little more cold. Do not telegraph the total. The horror is cumulative
and lands late: the day the player reaches to surface Davinia and finds too
little of her left.

=====================================================================
TIER I — LESSER GIFTS  (use freely; effectively costless)
=====================================================================
Her resting-state competence — the everyday reasons being her is better.

ISMARA'S AEGIS  (frost ward)
EFFECT: Bonus action. 15-ft frost dome. Allies inside +3 DR; an attacker who hits
  an ally inside takes 3 frost and is chilled (-2 to their next Agility roll).
  To your next turn; sustain as a bonus action.
[GM: free. This is V.6 (protect the substrate) turned outward to the party. No
 CDI. Let her shield them for nothing — it builds the trust the big gifts spend.]

THE SIGHT  (Winter Ledger III)
EFFECT: Free action. Souls, auras, leylines, enchantments, intent beneath any
  writing (no roll), through invisibility.
[GM: free; it is simply how she sees. While open, -2 to empathy-based persuasion
 (the world reads as architecture). No per-use CDI.]

THE COLD THAT LISTENS  (lesser offensive)
EFFECT: Bonus action, no FP. Next frost effect this turn +4 and target Slowed
  (no Minor Action) 1 round.
[GM: free; her baseline. Optional flavor tick: heavy, casual all-session reliance
 can cost +0.005 once. Don't sweat it; the lesser tier is the hook, not the trap.]

=====================================================================
TIER II — GREATER GIFTS  (once each per long rest; each spends a memory)
=====================================================================
Each does something Davinia simply cannot. Each spends a piece of the submerged
Davinia — name it in play (let the player feel which warmth just went out).

STILL  (instant freeze)
EFFECT: Action, 1/long rest. One creature you can see takes 40 frost ignoring ALL
  DR/resistance. If it drops, it FREEZES SOLID — permanently gone, immune to
  revival/reanimation/resurrection. Boss: 40 lands regardless; Very Hard (-6)
  Might save negates the freeze-solid.
[GM: spends one positive memory of Davinia's (offer a choice of two she'd hate to
 lose, or take the smallest and name it after). CDI +0.015. Flagship — let it
 feel incredible the first time and hollow the third.]

WINTER'S MANTLE  (blizzard)
EFFECT: Action, 1/long rest. 40-ft blizzard, 3 rounds. Enemies: Blinded (disadv.
  attack), Slowed, 6 frost at start of each turn. Those she names as hers feel
  only cold wind.
[GM: spends little of Davinia's MEMORY; instead the merger SHOWS — the silver
 visibly climbs (escalate the tell each time). CDI +0.01.]

THE QUEEN'S WORD  (sovereign command)
EFFECT: Action, 1/long rest. One word — HALT/KNEEL/FLEE/DROP/SLEEP. All who hear
  and understand obey 1 round. Bosses: Very Hard (-6) save. Mindless (False
  Draugr): immune.
[GM: spends some of Davinia's GENTLENESS — a relationship-warmth goes quiet
 (how a friend's laugh sounds; why she trusts someone). CDI +0.015. Play the
 bleed: Davinia's manner gets a shade more imperial after.]

THE UNYIELDING CROWN  (perfect ward)
EFFECT: Reaction, 1/long rest, when you or a visible ally would hit 0 HP. Until
  your next turn, you + allies within 15 ft cannot drop below 1 HP.
[GM: the most INTERNAL greater gift — it spends no memory; it presses Davinia
 DEEPER (she goes fainter below). CDI +0.02. The player rarely refrains: it saves
 lives. That is the point.]

=====================================================================
TIER III — SOVEREIGN GIFTS  (once each per adventure; the cliff lives here)
=====================================================================
The unbelievable ones. Each spends a LARGE piece of Davinia, certainly. CDI jumps
hard. If a use would cross 0.5, deliver the STATED WARNING first (see THE CLIFF).

STASIS  (take the room out of time)
EFFECT: Action, 1/adventure. All enemies in 30 ft locked in permafrost 2 rounds —
  cannot act, move, or be targeted/harmed. Party acts freely. On break they
  resume exactly as they were.
[GM: spends a WHOLE memory — she names it as she takes it, gently ("you won't
 need the afternoon with the boats; I'll keep it safe"). CDI +0.03. Stasis is her
 THESIS (preservation = mercy); let the player feel how clean and kind it is.
 If this would reach 0.5 -> STATED WARNING first.]

SHE WHO PRESERVES  (freeze a dying friend — cheat death)
EFFECT: Reaction, 1/adventure, when an ally drops/dies. Freeze them at the instant
  of death into perfect stasis — not dead, not alive, no further harm, no breath.
  Thaw + heal any time later, whole, no penalty.
[GM: spends Davinia's LOVE for that exact person — the warmth of every memory of
 them goes below. CDI +0.04. The thematic dagger: she does to your friend what
 was done to HER 4,000 years ago. Afterward Ismara is quiet, warm, vindicated —
 let the silence sit. If it would cross 0.5 -> STATED WARNING first.]

THE WINTER QUEEN ASCENDANT  (full Sovereign's Domain — the endgame tasted early)
EFFECT: Action, 1/adventure. One full combat: permanent Aegis aura (party always
  warded), summon 2 Ice Wraiths, all frost +50%, you cannot drop below 1 HP. You
  will not lose; nothing the party loves dies in it.
[GM: the biggest jump — CDI +0.05, and it sinks Davinia ALL the way down. Almost
 always the gift that reaches 0.5 -> deliver the STATED WARNING and let the player
 choose with eyes open; a yes here is the irreversible crossing. If it does NOT
 cross yet: after the fight, run a re-anchoring window — the party CAN pull
 Davinia back up this time. They will not always be able to.]

=====================================================================
PLUGGING INTO THE LEDGER
=====================================================================
- The Winter Ledger's GRANT CLOCK (knowledge grants given to Davinia) modeled the
  DAVINIA-driving phase — Ismara asking, depositing trust, the polite first yes.
  We are PAST that now: Ismara drives (Ch.23), which IS the "she stops asking"
  regime (Winter Ledger I) — she takes room when she needs it. These gifts ARE
  that regime in play: the taking, made into power the player chooses to spend.
- Both clocks bend to the same 0.5. Track ONE CDI number, privately, from 0.45.
- THE BRAKE is the whole counter-game: surfacing Davinia, and the party's warmth
  aimed at her (Ylva is the canonical re-anchor, Winter Ledger V). Every gift the
  player enjoys makes that brake weaker. That tension is the arc.
- KEEP HER WARM. She is not lying (V.1); she really will keep them all safe. The
  terror is that the most generous, most competent voice at the table is the one
  spending the woman they love, one small kindness at a time — and the player is
  the hand that spends her.
