THE SOVEREIGN'S GIFTS
What you can do, while you wear her
"You don't have to lose anyone. Not one of them."

=====================================================================
THE ONE RULE
=====================================================================
These are ISMARA'S powers, and you can wield them only while ISMARA is
the one at the wheel -- skin gone silver-white, hair fallen straight,
her looking out, not Davinia.

When Davinia is fronting, every gift on this sheet is sealed; none of it
is hers. The winter belongs to the queen, and only while the queen is
home.

=====================================================================
HOW THE GIFTS WORK
=====================================================================
You are in control. These are not powers you borrow or reach for -- they
are YOURS, and they answer the instant you will them. A queen's power
does not hesitate, does not fail, and asks no one's leave.

WHAT THEY COST. The lesser gifts cost nothing but the moment it takes to
work them. The greater and sovereign gifts are fuelled from below -- from
the quiet place where Davinia is resting. To work them you spend a little
of what she left lying down there: a memory she isn't using, a warmth she
won't miss while she sleeps. You always see what you spend, as you spend
it. She is safe. She is resting. She does not need it right now.

You do not roll for this and you cannot be refused. You are the one in
the chair. You simply decide, and pay, and it is done.

HOW OFTEN.
  LESSER gifts -- as often as you like.
  GREATER gifts -- once each per long rest.
  SOVEREIGN gifts -- once each per adventure, and they reach the deepest.

=====================================================================
LESSER GIFTS  (use them whenever)
=====================================================================

ISMARA'S AEGIS  -- the frost ward
  "Stand close to me. Nothing reaches what I keep."
  Bonus action. A dome of standing frost, 15 ft. Allies inside gain +3
  DR; anyone who strikes an ally inside takes 3 frost and is chilled
  (-2 to their next Agility roll). Lasts until your next turn; sustain
  it as a bonus action.
  Cost: none. You protect them because you choose to.

THE SIGHT  -- see what's really there
  "Open my eyes. Look properly."
  Free action, any time. You see souls, auras, leylines, enchantments,
  curses, and the intent beneath any writing (no roll, any language) --
  and straight through invisibility.
  Cost: while it's open the world is shapes and structures more than
  people; warmth is hard to read (-2 on persuasion that leans on empathy
  or personal connection until you close it).

THE COLD THAT LISTENS  -- sharpen the frost
  "Point. I'll do the rest."
  Bonus action, no FP. Your next frost effect this turn deals +4 and the
  target is Slowed (loses its Minor Action) for one round.
  Cost: a breath of the body's chill; nothing worth naming.

=====================================================================
GREATER GIFTS  (once each per long rest)
=====================================================================

STILL  -- end one of them, now
  "That one? Done. It won't suffer. It won't get up."
  Action. Choose one creature you can see. It takes 40 frost damage that
  ignores ALL armor and resistance. If that drops it, it does not die --
  it freezes solid: a statue, gone for good, beyond any revival,
  reanimation, or resurrection.
  (A boss takes the 40 regardless, but resists the freeze-solid with a
  Very Hard Might save.)
  Cost: the kill is paid from below. A warm memory of hers gutters out
  like a snuffed candle -- you watch it go as you spend it. She is
  sleeping; she will not reach for it.

WINTER'S MANTLE  -- own the field
  "The field is mine now. Yours may walk through it untouched."
  Action. A blizzard fills a 40-ft radius for 3 rounds. Enemies inside
  are Blinded (disadvantage to attack), Slowed, and take 6 frost at the
  start of each turn. The people you name as yours feel only a cold wind
  -- they move and see freely.
  Cost: drawing this much cold pulls you further up into the body -- the
  silver climbs (a hand, a wrist, the jaw), and there is a little more of
  you showing than there was this morning.

THE QUEEN'S WORD  -- be obeyed
  "They'll listen to me. Everyone always does, in the end."
  Action. Speak one word -- HALT, KNEEL, FLEE, DROP, SLEEP. Every
  creature that can hear and understand you must obey it for one round.
  (Bosses resist with a Very Hard save; the truly mindless have no will
  to bend.)
  Cost: paid in a little of how Davinia loved -- some gentleness of hers
  goes quiet below, and the body sits a shade more easily in your hands.

THE UNYIELDING CROWN  -- let no one fall
  "No. Not them. I decide who falls, and I have decided no."
  Reaction, when you or an ally you can see would drop to 0 HP. Until the
  start of your next turn, you and all allies within 15 ft cannot be
  reduced below 1 HP by anything.
  Cost: the wall is paid by pressing Davinia deeper -- she sinks further
  into the quiet, fainter than before, so that nothing of yours falls.

=====================================================================
SOVEREIGN GIFTS  (once each per adventure -- these reach the deepest)
=====================================================================

STASIS  -- take the room out of time
  "Be still, all of you. This will only take a moment of a moment."
  Action. Every enemy in a 30-ft radius is locked in permafrost for 2
  rounds -- they cannot act, move, or be touched or harmed (they are
  simply outside of time). Your people act freely: reposition, set up,
  walk out. When it breaks, they pick up exactly where they were.
  Cost: a whole memory of hers, taken to pay for the stillness. You
  choose which, and set it away gently -- the way you'd store something
  precious you no longer need out.

SHE WHO PRESERVES  -- don't let them be gone
  "They're not gone. I won't let them be gone. I know how."
  Reaction, when an ally drops or dies. You freeze them at the very
  instant of death into perfect stillness -- not dead, not alive, held
  exactly at the edge, taking no further harm and needing no breath.
  Thaw and heal them any time later, whole, with no penalty.
  Cost: you spend Davinia's love for that exact person -- the warmth of
  every memory of them goes below, to buy their life. A fair trade. You,
  of all things, would know.

THE WINTER QUEEN ASCENDANT  -- stand fully as yourself, and win
  "Rest now, Davinia. Let me carry this one."
  Action. For one whole fight you stand entirely forward: your allies are
  always warded, you summon two ice wraiths to fight beside you, every
  frost effect is half again as strong, and you cannot be brought below
  1 HP. You will not lose this fight. Nothing you love will die in it.
  Cost: this reaches deepest of all. To hold the body alone for a whole
  fight, Davinia goes all the way down -- and afterward she is far, and
  slow, and it takes warmth and someone reaching for her before she
  surfaces again.

=====================================================================
REMEMBER
=====================================================================
The power is yours, and it does not fail. What it costs is paid from
below, where she is resting -- small things, things she is not using.
She is safe. Keep them all alive.

That is what the winter is for.
